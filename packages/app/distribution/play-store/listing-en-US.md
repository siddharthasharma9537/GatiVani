## Full description (4000 char max)
GatiVani turns your morning newspaper into a podcast. Open the app, pick a newspaper, hit play — and listen on your commute, at the gym, or while making chai.

WHAT YOU GET
- Daily editions from leading Telugu newspapers, refreshed every morning.
- AI-generated, presenter-quality narration in natural Indic voices.
- Crisp two-sentence summaries — skim the day in 5 minutes, or dive in for the full read-out.
- Background playback with lock-screen controls and Bluetooth/CarPlay support.
- Bookmark articles, save offline, and resume across devices.
- Adjustable playback speed (0.75x – 2x) and sleep timer.

BUILT FOR INDIAN COMMUTERS
- Works on patchy 3G/4G — articles cache for offline listening.
- Low data mode: under 5 MB per full edition.
- Phone-call interruptions handled cleanly, audio resumes automatically.

PRIVACY-FIRST
- No ads. No tracking beyond crash and performance telemetry.
- Bookmarks and history sync only if you sign in.
- Open about what we collect — see https://gativani.app/privacy.

LANGUAGES
Initial release: Telugu. Tamil, Hindi, and Kannada in beta.

FEEDBACK
Email feedback@gativani.app — we read everything.
