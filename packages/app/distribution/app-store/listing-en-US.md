## Description (4000 char max)
GatiVani turns your morning newspaper into a podcast. Pick a newspaper, hit play, and listen on your commute, at the gym, or while making chai.

WHAT YOU GET
- Daily editions from leading Telugu newspapers, refreshed every morning.
- AI-generated, presenter-quality narration in natural Indic voices.
- Two-sentence summaries — skim the day in 5 minutes or hear the full read-out.
- Background playback, lock-screen controls, CarPlay support.
- Bookmark articles, save offline, resume across devices.
- Adjustable speed (0.75x – 2x) and sleep timer.

BUILT FOR COMMUTERS
- Works offline — articles cache after first load.
- Low-data mode under 5 MB per edition.
- Clean handling of phone-call interruptions.

PRIVACY-FIRST
- No ads, no tracking beyond crash and performance telemetry.
- Bookmarks and history sync only when signed in.
- Privacy policy: https://gativani.app/privacy

LANGUAGES
Telugu at launch. Tamil, Hindi, Kannada in beta.

Email feedback@gativani.app — we read everything.
